Graceful ladies sing and dance in life-like drama

On a jade stage carved of Tianhuang stone of blessing and longevity.

Drunk, I enjoy the beauties in my dream;

Awake, I still recollect their sweet singing.

—Shi Er Jin Chai Tu

(“Painting of the Twelve Beauties”)
