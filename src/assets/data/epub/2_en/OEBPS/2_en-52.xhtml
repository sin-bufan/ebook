As luminous as grease, greenish willows sway in drizzle;

Washed by rain, spring willows are reflected on the river;

Newly-gathered green bamboo shoots still bear dewdrops,

Unripe grapes remain transparently green.

The green will merge with the jade seal.

Money cannot buy vitality, no matter how much is spent.

——Qian Jin Cun Bi

(“An Inch of Jade Worth 1,000 Pieces of Gold”)
