Those monographs recount the history of seal stones from various perspectives, set standards of seal stones appreciation, showcase famous seal stones, and illustrate colorful stories about the relationship between man and seal stones. Through those monographs and books, we can reach the following conclusions: First, seal stones have been constantly explored and discovered, and people’s appreciation and knowledge of them have been increasingly enhanced, as evidenced by more kinds of seal stones being recorded in relevant monographs and new subcategories within the same kind of seal stone continuously being discovered. Second, those monographs reflect that seal stone resources are unrenewable and seem to move toward exhaustion. Third, seal stone culture has been enriched with each passing year, and seal stones are integrated with the artistic creations and spiritual world of seal cutters and seal knob sculptors, thus becoming an integral part of traditional Chinese art and culture that conveys their creators’ personal accomplishments, temperaments, styles and dispositions.
