At the turn of the 21st Century, as the Xileng Seal-Cutting Society celebrated the 100th anniversary of its founding, its bond with Qingtian stone eventually saw fruitful achievements. A hundred members created over 100 exquisite seals under the theme of “Qingtian Seal Stone Enjoys Millennium-old Reputation.” Though small, these seals are of artistic charm and superb craftsmanship and demonstrate the artist’s fondness for Qingtian stone. Nowadays, these seals are collected in the Qingtian Stone Carving Museum as witnesses of the innate relationship between the stone and the Zhejiang Seal-Cutting School.
