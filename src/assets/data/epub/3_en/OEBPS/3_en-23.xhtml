Huanggangliang

The Huanggangliang preserves abundant Quaternary glacial vestiges of a typical and complete shape in rich and diverse in types. Situated in the convergence area of floristic communities of Mongolia, northern China, and northeastern China, Huanggangliang displays a remarkable biodiversity, hence it is reputed as the “Gene Pool of Plant Diversity”. Moreover, the geo-region is a national forest park with high mountains and dense forests that are home to more than 40 species of wild animals.
