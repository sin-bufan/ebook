Arshihaty

Arshihaty literally means “Precipitous Rocks” in Mongolian. Atop the mountain ridges stands the Arshihaty Hoodoos, a granite landform rarely seen in the world. They form varied shapes and their horizontal texture suggests piled books, which testify to the vicissitudes the stone forest has undergone over time. The Arshihaty is not only a natural museum of granite landforms, but also an ideal destination for geological research and sightseeing.
