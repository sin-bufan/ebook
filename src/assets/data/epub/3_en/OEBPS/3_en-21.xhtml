Reshui

Lying some 30 kilometers north of Jingpeng Town, the hot springs dubbed as “Holy Water” or “Celestial Spring” cover one square kilometers. Historical records show that Emperor Kangxi of the Qing Dynasty (1644-1911) once bathed in a hot spring here that became known as the “Bathing Pool of Kangxi.” The thermal water comes out of structural fractures in the granite formations underground. With a temperature as high as 83 degrees Celsius, the spring water is rich in 47 kinds of trace elements such as fluorine and radon. It is believed that bathing regularly in these hot springs can benefit the skin, increase energy and enhance the physique.
