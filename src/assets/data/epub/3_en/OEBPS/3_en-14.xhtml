Ulan Butong

The Ulan Butong is noted for its picturesque grassland and wetland landscapes beyond the Great Wall. It is also known as the site of the famous “Battle of Ulan Butong” fought on Sept.3, 1690 between the forces of the Qing Dynasty and the Zunghar Khanate. The enthralling natural scenery has made the area a popular summer resort for tourists, vacationers and photographers. Furthermore, it serves as a renowned setting for movies and TV dramas. To date, more than 50 films and TV series have been shot here, including My Fair Princess and Romance of the Three Kingdoms.
