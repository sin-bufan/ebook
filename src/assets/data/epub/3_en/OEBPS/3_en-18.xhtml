Qingshan

The granite rocks formed in the Qingshan more than 100 million years ago gradually became a stone forest with rock formations in various shapes due to wind erosion, gravity and other exogenetic forces. What make the landscape more impressive are some 200 oval, round or semi-round potholes on the surface of the granite rocks that form a gently-rolling landscape. As witnesses of different geological periods, those granite potholes are the best in the world in terms of scale, diversity and preservation.
