Xar Moron

The 380-km-long Xar Moron River runs west-east through the geopark. It is not only the geomorphic boundary between the Greater Hinggan and Yanshanranges, but also the geological boundary of the Xingmeng Paleozoic Geosyncline and the North China Platform. Many relics of the Hongshan Culture (circa 4,000-3,000 BCE) are preserved in the terraced areas along the Xar Moron River and its branches, which record the long history of ethnic groups in northern China.
