Jeholornis

This was about 70 centimeters long and was the most primitive bird in the world. It retained an extremely-long tail like dinosaurs and well-developed paws and teeth.

Scientists found more than 50 plant seeds in the stomach of Jeholornis fossils. They inferred that it used to store the seeds for later digestion.

Living period: Early Cretaceous Period, about 125 million years ago

Habitat: Liaoning Province, China

Living habits: Herbivorous and arboreal; able to fly

Classification-Order: Aves; Family: Jeholornis
