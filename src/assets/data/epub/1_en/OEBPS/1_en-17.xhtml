Eomaia

This was about the size of a small mouse. It was 14 centimeters in length and weighed a little more than 20 grams. Who would have expected it to have been the earliest placental mammal? According to scientists, it might be the ancestor of, or be akin to, all viviparous mammals including human beings stretching back about 130 million years.

Living period: Early Cretaceous Period, about 125 million years ago

Habitat: Liaoning Province, China

Living habits: Herbivorous and quadrupedal locomotion

Classification-Order: Mammalia; Family: Eutheria
