Dongbeititan

Dongbeititan was more than 13 meters in length, was as high as a three-storied building, and weighed two tons or more. Don’t assume that this massive beast was ferocious. In fact, it was a vegetarian and docile.

Living period: Early Cretaceous Period, about 125 million years ago

Habitat: Liaoning Province, China

Living habits: Herbivorous and quadrupedal locomotion

Classification-Order: Saurischia; Sub-order: Sauropoda
