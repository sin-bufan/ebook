Microraptor

One of the smallest dinosaurs in the world, with a body only about 55–77 centimeters long. It had wings on both fore limbs and posterior limbs, so vividly called a “four-winged dinosaur”.

Generally, the micro-raptor was arboreal and a good hunter. It fed on fish and small animals.

Living period: Early Cretaceous Period, about 125 million years ago

Habitat: Liaoning Province, China

Living habits: Carnivorous and able to fly

Classification-Order: Saurischia; Suborder: Theropoda; Family: Dromaeosauridae
