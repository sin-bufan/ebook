Odonata

We’ve all seen the lovely dragonfly, but this time I saw a “dragonfly” much bigger than any I’d ever seen! Its wing was much bigger than the sole of an adult’s foot. It is the largest dragonfly in China and the fourth largest in the world.

Living period: Early Cretaceous Period, about 125 million years ago

Habitat: Liaoning Province, China

Living habits: Carnivorous and able to fly

Classification-Order: Insecta; Sub-order: Odonata
